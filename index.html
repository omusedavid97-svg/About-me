About Me

I am a cardiothoracic surgeon with a passion for helping patients heal. I plan and perform complex surgeries on the heart, lungs, and chest, collaborating closely with a multidisciplinary team to ensure each patient receives the best possible care. I work with precision and care to treat heart and chest conditions, and I value empathy, teamwork, and lifelong learning in my career.

At the hospital, I am actively involved in patient rounds, pre-operative consultations, and post-operative follow-up. I make time to listen to each patient’s concerns, explain treatment options clearly, and provide reassurance so they feel supported during every step of their care. My patients know me as friendly, approachable, and dedicated to their wellbeing.

I also take pride in teaching medical students and residents, helping them learn surgical techniques, clinical decision-making, and compassionate patient care. I enjoy mentoring young doctors, answering their questions, and guiding them through real cases so they grow into confident, skilled physicians.

Outside of medicine, I enjoy spending time with my favorite animals and sharing stories about them in this portfolio.
